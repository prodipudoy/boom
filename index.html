Medical electrode sheet

healthy and durable

The inner side of the medical electrode sheet has heat dissipation mesh, which can fully dissipate local heat, making it more convenient to use, and there is no discomfort even if it is worn for a long time. The three electrodes have one more driving electrode than the two electrodes, which is used to minimize the influence of the common mode voltage introduced by the power line and other interference sources. The use of this pin can keep the voltage between the patient and the device almost constant, which greatly improves the common-mode rejection performance.

Sterile material,

professional and assured

The shell material that is in direct contact with the skin is made of unique medical antibacterial plastic material, so that you can wear it for a long time without concern. This material has passed BioCleanAct, US FDA certification, South Korea FDA certification, and is widely used in medical equipment.
