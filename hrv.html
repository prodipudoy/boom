Welbing & Health

Heart rate variability (HRV) is a measure of cardiac autonomic nervous system function and has been associated with multiple cardiovascular diseases and risk factors.

In addition, HRV is a commonly used tool in assessing stress and recovery. Boom Heart HRV software products provide easy-to-use measurement applications and detailed HRV analysis tools for health and wellbeing professionals who want to use HRV assessment as part of their services.

Exercise & Sports

For Exercise and Sports Heart rate variability (HRV) is an accurate method to assess regulation of the cardiovascular system, thus providing objective information on physiological recovery.

HRV based recovery monitoring is used by professional athletes in training optimization, but can as well be used by sports enthusiasts or anyone interested in their wellbeing for monitoring training load and recovery. HRV scores may help you in evaluating the effects of training such as cumulative fatigue or improvements in cardiorespiratory fitness and stress tolerance
